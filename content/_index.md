WeichertLabs is where I document what I build, break, and learn.

The topics span IT infrastructure, cybersecurity, networking, AI, IoT,
home automation, and whatever else ends up on the workbench. Some of it
I know well. Some of it I'm figuring out as I go. Both kinds are worth
documenting — the things that worked, and the things that didn't.

The goal isn't to be a polished tutorial site. It's honest, hands-on
documentation of real projects and real experiments, shared in case
someone else finds it useful.
